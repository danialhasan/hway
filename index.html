Why HWAY?

What makes H WAY unique is our organization’s ability to not only provide exceptional quality kosher food at a reasonable price, but to provide it onsite at partner hotels across North America under the supervision of a Mashgiach.

This feature allows organizations to plan kosher trips and conferences at venues that otherwise don’t serve Kosher food; preventing them from being limited to the select few hotels or conference centres that have permanent kosher kitchens.

By working with us, our partners are able to broaden their potential venue selection which usually translates into a better hotel choice and lower overall costs.

Services

H WAY is an integrated kosher trip planning solution. With both kosher event planning and
separate kosher catering divisions, H WAY is able to help with any and all of your kosher
needs no matter where you want to go.

Hotel Accomodation

H WAY has negotiated rates with a network of pre-approved “kosher-friendly” properties across North America. Let us utilize these relationships to help get you the best rate.

Kosher Catering

H WAY provides cost effective Glatt Kosher Catering services for groups of 50 or more in cities near and far in Canada & the United States.

Transportation

H WAY can handle all of your group transportation needs including arranging airfare and motor coach charters as well as local transportation within your destination city. Allow us to use our travel network to access itineraries and rates that are not otherwise available to the general public.

Prayer Facilities

H WAY can provide facilities for prayers at our partner properties & hotels. When necessary, we can arrange for it to be outfitted with a Machitza as well as a Torah.
